Hidden places near Indore

Mohadi Waterfall

The Mohadi Waterfall is located at a distance of 30 kms from Indore, offers a breathtaking sight of water falling from a height of about 250 feet amid a verdant valley.

Junapani Waterfall

Junapani Waterfalls is located near Indore, within a 30 km radius. It's a beautiful spot known for its natural beauty and serene environment

Shitlamata Waterfall

Sitlamata Waterfall is cascading down from a height of about 500 feet and is one of the most famous waterfalls near Indore. It is visited by a large number of tourists as well as devotees due to the presence of Sheetla Mata Temple.
